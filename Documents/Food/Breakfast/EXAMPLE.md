# Example breakfast

A full-day vegetarian + cheese plan designed to naturally hit a 3,000+ mg potassium target (healthy target)

> No need of extra (potassium) supplements.

---

### Breakfast (8:00 AM)

* 1 cup cooked oats -> 300 mg
* ½ cup blueberries -> 55 mg
* Handful of walnuts (~28 g) -> 125 mg

Breakfast total: ~480 mg

---

### Mid-Morning Snack (optional, 10:30 AM)

* 1 medium banana -> 420 mg

Snack total: 420 mg

---

### Lunch (1:00 PM)

* 1 cup cooked lentils -> 730 mg
* 1 cup raw spinach -> 160 mg
* 1 cup cherry tomatoes -> 400 mg
* 50 g cheese -> 60 mg

Lunch total: ~1,350 mg

---

### Afternoon Snack (4:00 PM)

* 1 cup plain yogurt -> 380 mg
* Handful of almonds (~28 g) -> 200 mg

Snack total: ~580 mg

---

### Dinner (7:00 PM)

* 1 cup cooked rice -> 55 mg
* 1.5 cups cooked edamame -> 1,000 mg
* 1 cup steamed broccoli -> 230 mg

Dinner total: ~1,285 mg

---

### Evening Snack (10:00 PM)

Helps to overcome a 8 hour long fast (sleeping)

* Handful of walnuts -> 125 mg
* Optional: half banana -> +210 mg

Evening total: 125–335 mg

---

### Daily Total

* Without the optional half banana: ~3,000 mg 
* With optional half banana: ~3,200–3,300 mg 

---

### Why this works

* Potassium: hits or slightly exceeds recommended daily intake
* Magnesium: multiple sources (walnuts, almonds, spinach) -> helps muscles and nerves
* Calcium: cheese + yogurt -> bone & muscle support
* Protein: lentils, edamame, nuts, cheese -> muscle repair
* Evening walnut snack: supports muscle relaxation overnight
