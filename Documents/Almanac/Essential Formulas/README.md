# Quick Reference: Common Formulas & Constants

## Physical Constants

| Concept | Formula/Value | Notes |
|---------|---------------|-------|
| Speed of Light (c) | 299,792,458 m/s ≈ 3×10⁸ m/s | In vacuum; approximately 300,000 km/s or 186,000 mi/s |
| Speed of Sound | ~343 m/s (1,235 km/h or 767 mph) at 20°C | Varies with temperature: v ≈ 331 + 0.6T (where T is °C) |
| Gravity (g) | 9.8 m/s² (Earth surface) | Often approximated as 10 m/s² for quick calculations |
| Pi (π) | 3.14159265... | Commonly used as 3.14 or 22/7 |
| Euler's Number (e) | 2.71828... | Base of natural logarithms |
| Golden Ratio (φ) | 1.618033... | (1 + √5) / 2 |

## Weather & Atmospheric

| Concept | Formula/Value | Notes |
|---------|---------------|-------|
| Thunder Distance | Distance (km) = Seconds / 3 | Count seconds between lightning and thunder; divide by 5 for miles |
| Lightning Speed | ~220,000 km/h (1/3 speed of light) | Thunder travels at speed of sound |
| Temperature Conversion | °F = (°C × 9/5) + 32; °C = (°F - 32) × 5/9 | Kelvin: K = °C + 273.15 |
| Heat Index Estimate | Feels hotter when humidity >60% | Rule: Each 10% humidity increase feels like ~1°F warmer above 80°F |
| Wind Chill Rough | Feels colder with wind | At 0°C with 50 km/h wind feels like -10°C |

## Basic Trigonometry

| Concept | Formula/Value | Notes |
|---------|---------------|-------|
| Sine (sin) | sin(θ) = opposite/hypotenuse | In right triangle |
| Cosine (cos) | cos(θ) = adjacent/hypotenuse | In right triangle |
| Tangent (tan) | tan(θ) = opposite/adjacent = sin/cos | In right triangle |
| Pythagorean Theorem | a² + b² = c² | c is hypotenuse of right triangle |
| SOH-CAH-TOA | Sine=Opp/Hyp, Cos=Adj/Hyp, Tan=Opp/Adj | Memory aid for trig ratios |
| Common Angles | sin(30°)=0.5, sin(45°)≈0.707, sin(60°)≈0.866 | cos(30°)≈0.866, cos(45°)≈0.707, cos(60°)=0.5 |
| Radian Conversion | Radians = Degrees × π/180 | 180° = π radians; 360° = 2π radians |
| Circle Circumference | C = 2πr = πd | r=radius, d=diameter |
| Circle Area | A = πr² | r=radius |

## Motion & Physics

| Concept | Formula/Value | Notes |
|---------|---------------|-------|
| Distance | d = v × t | Distance = velocity × time |
| Acceleration | a = (v - u) / t | a=acceleration, v=final velocity, u=initial velocity, t=time |
| Force | F = m × a | Newton's 2nd Law; mass × acceleration |
| Kinetic Energy | KE = ½mv² | m=mass, v=velocity |
| Potential Energy | PE = mgh | m=mass, g=gravity, h=height |
| Work | W = F × d | Force × distance (in direction of force) |
| Power | P = W/t = E/t | Work or energy per unit time |
| Momentum | p = m × v | Mass × velocity |
| Impulse | J = F × t = Δp | Force × time = change in momentum |
| Free Fall Distance | d = ½gt² | Distance fallen after t seconds (starting from rest) |
| Free Fall Velocity | v = gt | Velocity after t seconds of free fall |

## Electricity & Electronics

| Concept | Formula/Value | Notes |
|---------|---------------|-------|
| Ohm's Law | V = I × R | Voltage = Current × Resistance |
| Power (Electrical) | P = V × I = I²R = V²/R | Watts = Volts × Amps |
| Series Resistance | R_total = R₁ + R₂ + R₃ + ... | Resistances add in series |
| Parallel Resistance | 1/R_total = 1/R₁ + 1/R₂ + 1/R₃ + ... | For two: R = (R₁×R₂)/(R₁+R₂) |
| Battery Life | Hours = mAh / mA | Battery capacity divided by current draw |

## Area & Volume

| Concept | Formula/Value | Notes |
|---------|---------------|-------|
| Rectangle Area | A = length × width | Also for square: A = s² |
| Triangle Area | A = ½ × base × height | Or use Heron's formula for 3 sides |

| Sphere Volume | V = 4/3 πr³ | r=radius |
| Sphere Surface Area | A = 4πr² | r=radius |
| Cylinder Volume | V = πr²h | r=radius, h=height |

| Cone Volume | V = 1/3 πr²h | One-third of cylinder |
| Cube Volume | V = s³ | s=side length |
| Cube Surface Area | A = 6s² | Six square faces |
| Rectangular Prism Volume | V = l × w × h | Length × width × height |

## Probability & Statistics

| Concept | Formula/Value | Notes |
|---------|---------------|-------|
| Mean (Average) | x̄ = Σx / n | Sum of values divided by count |
| Probability | P(event) = favorable outcomes / total outcomes | Value between 0 and 1 |
| Percentage | Percentage = (part/whole) × 100 | To find what % one number is of another |
| Percent Change | % change = [(new - old) / old] × 100 | Positive = increase, negative = decrease |
| Compound Interest | A = P(1 + r/n)^(nt) | P=principal, r=rate, n=compounds/year, t=years |
| Simple Interest | I = P × r × t | P=principal, r=rate, t=time |
