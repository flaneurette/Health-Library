# Smart home improvement tips

---

## Health, Energy & Climate Control (Lower Bills + More Comfort)

### 0) UV blocking window film (clear film, not tinted)

* Blocks 99% of UV rays. No indoor tanning, no UV fading of objects and less risk of early wrinkels and skin cancer. Window film can last up to 20 years. Top tier improvement. 

### 1) Thermal Curtains

* Adds insulation in winter and blocks heat in summer (cuts heating/cooling costs).

### 2) Door Draft Stoppers / Weatherstripping

* Simple foam/brush strips that seal gaps under doors and windows.
* Reduces heat loss and cold drafts in winter.

### 3) Programmable / Smart Thermostat

* Automatically lowers heating/cooling when youâ€™re away or asleep.
* Saves significant energy overtime.

### 4) Reflective Roof Coating / Attic Insulation

* A cool roof coating reflects heat in summer.
* Proper attic insulation drastically reduces both heating and cooling needs.

---

## Air Quality & Health (Better Breathing, Lower Allergies)

### 5) High-Quality Air Filters (HEPA)

* For HVAC or portable air purifiers - reduces dust, pollen, smoke.
* Replace regularly to keep efficiency high.

### 6) Ventilation Fans / Heat Recovery Ventilators (HRV)

* Removes stale air and replaces it with fresh air while retaining heat.
* Great for airtight homes.

### 7) Dehumidifier / Humidifier

* Keeps indoor humidity in the healthy zone (40-60%).
* Prevents mold growth and respiratory irritation.

---

## Electricity Savings & Safety

### 8) LED Bulbs

* Long-lasting, low energy - big savings over old incandescent/halogen bulbs.

### 9) Smart Power Strips

* Prevent "vampire power draw" from idle electronics.
* Cuts phantom energy waste.

### 10) Surge Protectors

* Protect electronics from voltage spikes (especially useful in lightning season).

---

## Home Safety & Preparedness

### 11) Fire Extinguishers & Smoke/CO Detectors

* Replace batteries yearly; detectors save lives.
* CO detectors are especially important with gas appliances.

### 12) Emergency Kit (Water, Food, First-Aid)

* A small "go bag" plus a home kit prepares you for unexpected outages or weather events.

### 13) Battery Backup / UPS for Modem & Router

* Keeps your internet and devices running during short outages.

---

## Sun Protection (Like Window Film)

### 17) Outdoor Awnings

* Blocks sun and heat before it hits the glass.

### 18) UV Clothing & Hats / Sunscreen

* Protects skin year-round - lowers skin damage risk.

### 19) Tinted Car Windows (Where Legal)

* Keeps car cooler and reduces interior fading.

---

## Kitchen & Household Savings

### 20) Reusable Water Filter Pitcher

* Better tasting water and fewer bottled plastic bottles.

### 21) Silicone Food Storage / Beeswax Wraps

* Replaces single-use plastic - saves money and reduces waste.

### 22) Pressure Cooker / Multicooker

* Cuts cooking time and energy use.

### 23) Low-Flow Showerhead / Faucet Aerators

* Reduces water use without losing pressure.

---

## Forward-Thinking Tech & Tools

### 24) Portable Solar Charger

* Great for trips and emergency power.

### 25) Quality Tools (e.g., Drill, Ratchets, Screwdriver Set)

* Small repairs become DIY - no service calls.

### 26) Rechargeable Batteries + Charger

* Saves $$$ over disposables for remotes, toys, tools.

---

## Long-Term Comfort & Savings

### 27) Thermal Insulation for Pipes

* Prevents heat loss and protects against freezing in winter.

### 28) Exterior Shutters or Shade Screens

* Blocks sun early in the day - cuts cooling loads.

### 29) Gardening / Edible Plants

* Herbs, greens, tomatoes - fresh food with low ongoing cost.

### 30) Rain Barrels / Water Collection

* For garden use - lowers water consumption during dry months.
